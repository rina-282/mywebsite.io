GENERAL DISCIPLINE

At Sarhad School CBSE, we believe that discipline is a cornerstone of academic success and personal growth. Our approach to general discipline extends beyond traditional boundaries, fostering an environment where students learn to manage their time, emotions, and responsibilities effectively.

What is General Discipline?
General discipline involves the ability to self-regulate and maintain focus on goals, contributing to a structured and productive learning experience. It encompasses:
  1. Self-Management: Develop skills to manage their time and prioritize tasks, ensuring they meet deadlines and achieve their academic goals.
  2. Personal Accountability: We encourage students to take ownership of their actions and decisions, fostering a culture of responsibility.
  3. Goal-Oriented Mindset: Setting clear, achievable goals, students learn to navigate challenges with purpose and determination.
  4. Attendance: Attendance is crucial for academic success. Students are expected to arrive on time and attend all scheduled classes unless excused for legitimate reasons.
  5. Dress Code: Should adhere to the school dress code, which promotes a respectful and focused learning environment.
