School Profile

Home / School Profile


VISION

Enabling all learners to recognize their worth, to faster creative thinking, and to play an active role in society, in its many facts. “We picture education not simply as an acquisition of knowledge, but rather as an experience, which teaches all people how to grow and think.”

MISSION

"Our goal is to spread a love of learning by creating an atmosphere where everyone's voice is valued and inquiry is encouraged. Our goal is to develop creative thinkers who welcome challenges and pursue knowledge outside of the classroom. Our goal is to establish a thriving ecosystem that promotes innovation, diversity, and resilience through cooperative relationships with families and the community. We give students the tools they need to become critical thinkers and compassionate leaders who can successfully negotiate the challenges of the real world and contribute significantly to society."
