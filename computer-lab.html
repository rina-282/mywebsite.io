Welcome to the Computer Lab

Equipped with the latest technology to empower your academic journey

Lab Features & Services

Cutting-Edge Technology and Equipment

Our computer lab is equipped with high-performance computers for demanding tasks like programming, video editing, and design work.

Wide Range of Software

The lab offers essential software, including Microsoft Office, Adobe Creative Cloud, and tools for programming like Python, Java, and MATLAB.

High-Speed Internet

Enjoy reliable high-speed internet access for research, online classes, or collaborative projects.

Printing and Scanning Services

Convenient on-site printing and scanning services for all your academic needs.

Technical Assistance

Lab assistants are ready to help with troubleshooting or software issues during operating hours.

Quiet and Productive Environment

The lab offers a designated quiet zone for focused study and project work.

Access and Rules

A valid ID is required for access. Workstations are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Reservations can be made for group projects.

Operating Hours

Open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Closed on Sundays.
